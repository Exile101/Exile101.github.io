Designing

A full complement of customizing services gives you the flexibility you need to achieve the exact look you want. Our capabilities are virtually endless and include logo embroidery, screen printing, emblems, stripes and complete custom design & manufacturing programs. Pricing

Our customers vary in size from small local businesses to big corporate. To make sure all customers receive the best possible pricing, we utilize several discount levels based on quantity ordered per style. We also offer special pricing incentives for larger and national accounts.

Quality

Quality is a central topic, from initial idea to final product. It is our aim to always exceed our customers’ expectations on quality. Paying attention to what matters: Fabric, Fit, Construction and Durability, we strive to offer inspiring fashion with unbeatable value for money. We conduct all our activities with a sole aim to provide the most qualitative range of products to our clients.
