About me

Hey guys! I'm Suman. I'm a kolkata based web developer. I started developing for the web since my freshmen year in college,that is around 3 years back. Ever since I have worked on a number of projects which include websites for a few startups, the website for Mukti 2015(A three days long meet for celebrating the freedom of open source), some freelance works, and quite a few personal projects. I started developing as a past-time but later on I found out I could land a few decent pages and earn some reputation (and ofcourse cash!) in the process.So I'm basically a senior chemical engineering student of National Institute of Technology Durgapur who develops for the web.
